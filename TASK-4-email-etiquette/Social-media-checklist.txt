# Responsible Social Media Use Checklist

## Do’s:
 Think before you post – what you share online stays there  
 Protect your privacy – set things up carefully  
 Verify before sharing – check facts and where they come from  
 Be respectful – keep conversations positive  
 Secure your account – use a strong password and two-factor authentication  

## Don’ts:
 Don’t share sensitive information – keep personal information private
 Don’t spread misinformation – fake news can really harm people
 Don’t bully others online – be respectful to others online
 Don’t trust strangers so easily – be careful about strangers online
 Don’t overshare – keep your personal life balanced and secure
