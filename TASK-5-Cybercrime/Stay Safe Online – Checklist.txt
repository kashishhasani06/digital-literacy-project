Stay Safe Online – Checklist:
Do not click on unknown or suspicious links received via SMS, email, or social media.
Always check website URLs carefully (look for “https://” and correct spelling) before entering any details.
Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking, shopping, or logging into important accounts.
Never share OTPs, passwords, or PINs with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
Download apps only from trusted sources like official app stores.
Never approve unknown UPI collect/payment requests.
Do not scan random QR codes sent by strangers or found online.
Always double-check receiver details before sending money via UPI.
Be cautious of urgent or threatening messages asking for immediate action.
Keep your phone and apps updated with the latest security patches.
Log out from shared or public devices after use.
